A comprehensive platform to streamline your car rental business operations

Our system offers a range of features tailored to meet the needs of car rental businesses and individual vehicle owners. Whether you manage a large fleet or a few cars, our platform can help you efficiently manage bookings, track vehicle maintenance, and analyze performance.

Key Features

Add Cars

Easily add and manage vehicles in your rental fleet with details such as make, model, and registration plate and more.

Add Contacts

Keep track of your customers by adding them to your contacts list. Manage their bookings and track spending for better customer analysis and relationship management.

Add Groups

Organize your clients, such as hotels and airports, into groups for easier management and tracking of bookings. With groups, you can calculate and keep track of commissions for each organization, making it easy to manage your business relationships.

Make Bookings

Effortlessly create and manage bookings using cars, contacts, and groups, with options for notes, scheduling, pricing, and tracking.

Manage Maintenance

Keep your fleet in top condition by tracking and scheduling maintenance tasks such as MOT, insurance, and road tax.

View Statistics

Gain insights into the performance of your vehicles and bookings with detailed statistics and analytics.

Download Excel Files

Export your data, including bookings, cars, and contacts, into Excel files for easy analysis and reporting.

Calendar View

Visualize your bookings in a convenient calendar view for easy tracking and scheduling, the calendar is also downloadable.
